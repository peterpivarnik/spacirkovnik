## 2. Data We Collect

The Špacírkovník app collects only the data strictly necessary for its operation:

- **Email address** — obtained when you sign in or register (via Google account or email and password).
- **User identifier (UID)** — an anonymous ID generated by Firebase.
- **List of activated games** — which games you have access to.
- **Device location** — used *only during active app use* for map display and route navigation. Location is **never stored** on our servers.

---

## 3. Purpose of Processing

- **Email and UID** — to identify you and link your account to activated games.
- **Activations** — to grant access to games you are entitled to.
- **Location** — solely to display the map and calculate the route during gameplay.
- **Email (marketing)** — to send news and offers (new games, discounts), **only if you give consent** (legal basis: consent, Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR).

> **Marketing communication is optional.** We send news and offers **only** to users who gave explicit consent (e.g. by ticking a box during registration). You can **withdraw consent at any time** — via the unsubscribe link in every email or by writing to info@spacirkovnik.sk. Withdrawing consent does not affect your use of the app or purchased games.

---

## 4. Third-Party Services

We use the following service providers:

- **Google Firebase** (Google Ireland Limited) — authentication, activation database, and game image storage.
- **Mapbox** (Mapbox, Inc.) — map display and route calculation.

We do **not sell** data to third parties and do **not use** any advertising or analytics SDKs.

---

## 5. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. Upon account deletion, your data (email, UID, activations) is removed from our systems.

---

## 6. Your Rights (GDPR)

You have the following rights under GDPR:

- Right of access to your data
- Right to rectification
- Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten")
- Right to restriction of processing
- Right to data portability
- Right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority ([Office for Personal Data Protection of the Slovak Republic](https://dataprotection.gov.sk))

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at info@spacirkovnik.sk.

---

## 7. Security

Data is transmitted over HTTPS and stored in the secure Google Firebase infrastructure. Database access rules ensure each user can only read their own activations.

---

## 8. Children

The app is designed for families, including children. If the user is under 16, sign-in and use of the app should take place under parental or guardian supervision.
